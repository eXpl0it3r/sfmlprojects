Title: About

----

Intro: While the SFML forum provides a section for (link: https://en.sfml-dev.org/forums/index.php?board=10.0 text: projects), it happens too often that nice projects get lost in the depth of that sub-forum or that the archive with the game gets deleted. With the SFML Projects website we want to provide a platform to host, share and archive projects that were made with or for (link: http://www.sfml-dev.org/ text: SFML).

----

Text:

### Contributions

This project is made for the community by the community, as such we'll try to include everyone who wants to help out in any way.

* The project is open source and anyone who has an idea is free to start up a discussion on IRC or Twitter and provide pull requests.
* We're always looking for people who want to help build out the current list of games, tools and libraries.

If any of this sounds interesting to you, don't hesitate to get in touch!
